Who am I?

Lover of new technologies and popularizer of science.
Welcome to my world!

Currently, I'm studying for my Master's degree in mechatronics at Kazimierz Wielki University
in Bydgoszcz. My main scientific interests are related to cybersecurity. In my free time, I try to understand quantum physics and the structure and functioning of the brain.

The main goal of this website is to share my work. You can expect in near futre that I will upload on the website projects that I have done in the past and also projects on which I am currently working on.
I believe that together we can provide and exchange ideas about specific problems in technology and society.

Below you can find contact to me. Suggestions, advice and feedback are always welcome!
